Dr. Jeremy Jung is a highly trained Foot and Ankle Surgeon with experience treating a wide variety of foot and ankle conditions.

He completed his undergraduate degree in Kinesiology and Health Sciences from the University of British Columbia, and later received his Doctorate of Podiatric Medicine at the California School of Podiatric Medicine, graduating with academic honors. Jeremy then completed his podiatric surgical residency at the Advent Health System in Orlando Florida (formerly Florida Hospital), a busy level 1 trauma center where he served as chief resident. Here he received extensive training in foot and ankle reconstruction, lower extremity trauma, and diabetic limb salvage.

When not treating patients, he enjoys playing hockey, exploring and hiking with wife and his dog Henley, and enjoying all the wonderful food Vancouver has to offer.
